Having spent many years at sea, Tom is an expert in navigation and pirates. The victim of a mutiny in October 2015, Tom found himself marooned on a small island in the Irish sea with only a laptop to his name. Resigning himself to making the best of a bad situation, he settled himself in a local tavern where he worked behind the bar for a bed, bread and internet time. On the blustery evening of October 11, he found himself sitting in front of the Founders and Coders website and decided to give coding a crack. Three months of head-scratching later, he was clean shaven and on a train to London to start a new life as a code warrior!
